Perfect Movement Law → Plasma Stability Law

Perfect Movement Law ⇔ Long-lived, low-drift bn-orbits inside the reactor geometry

  • preserve their energy distribution in controlled ways.

This leads to a concrete design principle:

In practice, this means scanning parameters (coil geometry, current, pressure profiles) and measuring a Funebra score:

Lorbit = number of stable turns of a bn-point before escape or chaos

Maximizing Lorbit across many bn-points is equivalent to making a more stable fusion configuration.
