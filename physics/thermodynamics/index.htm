What is “Funebra Thermodynamics”?

In classical physics, thermodynamics studies how heat, energy and entropy move through matter and time. In the Funebra universe, we extend this idea: every portion of heat is also a movement of bn-points – tiny carriers of memory, intention and geometry.

When you read or hear “Funebra thermodynamics”, it does not refer to a standard, established academic sub-field. It is a conceptual layer on top of physics – a way to talk about the emotional, symbolic and geometric side of heat and entropy.

Very loosely, you can think of it as:

  • Heat → how strongly Funebra points vibrate together.
  • Temperature → how fast a pattern wants to change its shape.
  • Entropy → how many stories a configuration of bn-points can tell.

The pages linked on the right are not scientific papers. They are math-art documents, mapping physics language into Funebra language – to keep the door open for future formal work.
