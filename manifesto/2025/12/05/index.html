1. The Core Funebra Triad: Page • Cards • Play

We started by capturing existence, math, and life as a simple game metaphor. In Funebra language:

Funebra Formula Page • Cards • Play
  • Existence = The Page — the blank, infinite manifold that can host any pattern.
  • Math = The Cards — every rule, geometry, symmetry and function drawn onto the page.
  • Life = The Play — the traversal of cards, the path a living system takes through the grid.

Compressed form:

“Page = BE • Cards = KNOW • Life = MOVE.”

Funebra shorthand for the basic ontology.

Illustration — Life Traversing the Grid

Think of the page as a 2D or n-D grid of bn-points. Each card is a local law or pattern; life is a token moving across these tiles in time, sampling the structure.

  • Page: the coordinate system / state space.
  • Cards: allowed moves and interactions.
  • Play: actual history taken through that space.

2. Mapping Funebra to Modern Science

You asked for proof in mainstream terms. We translated the Funebra metaphor to physics, thermodynamics and information theory:

Funebra Concept Scientific View Illustrative Notes
Existence = Page Universe as a state space (spacetime, phase space, Hilbert space) in which all physical states live. A “blank” manifold that doesn’t decide outcomes; it just allows any physically possible configuration to exist.
Math = Cards Mathematical laws of nature: equations, symmetries, invariants that constrain dynamics. The “unreasonable effectiveness” of math: equations designed for curiosity end up describing reality with shocking precision.
Life = Play Far-from-equilibrium processes (dissipative structures), plus Darwinian evolution and information processing. Life “rides” energy flows and uses information to keep its pattern going against entropy.
Memory Information stored in physical states (neurons, DNA, external records). Information is physical (Landauer). The “save file” of the play—how patterns persist across time and can be re-loaded or reconstructed.
Player Conscious agent or controller that selects actions based on internal models and goals. The algorithm of will, minimizing surprise/free energy, exploring the landscape of possible moves.
Main Game App The reality engine: Standard Model, gravitation, quantum fields, all running together. Physics as the operating system on which all Funebra “apps” (worlds, simulations, lives) are executed.
Science Documentation of the game, written by players who observe and formalize patterns. Science doesn’t create rules; it reverse-engineers and publishes the patch notes of reality.
Self-Being Meta-consciousness: a system that knows it is playing, and can reflect on its own state. Identity arises when information loops back and models itself modelling itself.
Resurrection Re-instantiation of a pattern: restoring information and state so that the process can continue. Resurrection = loading a save: not reversing time, but reconstructing the structure that once moved.
